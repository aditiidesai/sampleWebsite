Things to know about upgrade options

Flight Experience

Virgin Australias Business Class offer an all-inclusive flight experience, with Priority services and complimentary in-flight benefits.

Upgrades

Upgrades to your Virgin Australia booking may be requested at any time after booking your flight up to a few hours prior to departure.

Waitlist

If there are no upgrade seats available at the time of your request, you may be able to waitlist for an upgrade.

Eligibility rules apply.

Availability

Flight upgrades are available on Virgin Australia flights.

Lounge Access

Relax in one of Virgin Australias 7 domestic lounges.

Note: Virgin Australia currently do not operate any international lounges.

From 1 July 2025, all Velocity members travelling on Economy Flex fares, and Gold and Platinum Velocity members travelling on Economy Choice fares will be eligible to request to use Velocity Points to upgrade to Business Class on Virgin Australia flights between Australia and Doha (VA 1 -29), subject to availability.

If your request for an upgrade isn't successful at the time because of availability, you can request to be placed on a waitlist in the event upgrades become available.

How to UpgradeMe

You can request to upgrade your Virgin Australia operated flight after you have made your booking.1

For Virgin Australia Domestic and International Short Haul Flights:
  • Online: until four hours prior to the scheduled flight departure time
  • Via Our Membership Contact Centre: until two hours prior to the scheduled flight departure time (a fee may apply) - Call us now!
  • At Domestic Check-in: until 30 minutes prior to the scheduled flight departure time. See one of our friendly ground crew for assistance.
  • At International Check-in: until 60 minutes prior to the scheduled flight departure time.
  • In a Virgin Australia domestic Lounge: on the same-day of travel for a Virgin Australia domestic sector (times differ depending on your tier, see important information below to find out more)
  • For Platinum members, via Virgin Australia's Guest Contact Centre: until two hours prior to the scheduled flight departure time. Call the Guest Contact Centre on 13 67 89.
Upgrade online
From 1 July 2025 - For Virgin Australia International Long Haul flights between Australia and Doha (VA 1-29):
  • Via Our Membership Contact Centre: until 24 hours prior to the scheduled flight departure time (a fee may apply).
  • For Platinum members, via Virgin Australia's Guest Contact Centre: until 24 hours prior to the scheduled flight departure time. Call the Guest Contact Centre on 13 67 89.

Please note, there are currently no options to request an upgrade online, at the airport or during check-in.

    • If you meet the eligibility requirements for UpgradeMe Points but are unable to secure a confirmed upgrade, you can register to waitlist by accessing your booking on virginaustralia.com, or calling the Membership Contact Centre at any time from confirmation and ticketing of your booking:
      • For Virgin Australia's domestic or short haul international flights: until 4 hours before the scheduled flight departure
      • For Virgin Australia's long haul international flights between Australia and Doha (VA 1-29): until 24 hours before the scheduled flight departure
    • You must have enough Points in your Velocity account at the time the waitlist is requested and for the duration of the waitlist period for an Upgrade to be successful.

    • Points are not deducted from your account until the Upgrade is confirmed.

    • For domestic and short haul international flights, we commence the confirming of Upgrades for members on our UpgradeMe Points waitlist 5 days prior to flight departure.
    • For long haul international flights between Australia and Doha (VA 1-29), we confirm upgrades for members on our UpgradeMe Points waitlist when seating availability becomes available, until 24 hours prior to flight departure.
    • We prioritise our domestic or international short haul waitlist requests by Velocity membership tier, providing the highest level of priority to Platinum members, followed by Gold, Silver and Red. So the higher your tier, the sooner you may have your Upgrade request confirmed.
    • We will prioritise our international long haul flights between Australia and Doha (VA 1-29) requests by fare type, followed by Velocity membership Status:
      • The highest level of priority will be extended to members that have purchased an Economy Flex fare and then prioritised by Velocity membership level. I.e., Platinum members that have purchased a Flex fare will have priority, followed by Gold, then Silver and then Red.
      • The next level of priority will extended to Platinum and Gold members that have purchased an Economy Choice fare, with Platinum members having higher priority than Gold members.
      • Please note, Membership level is validated both at time of requesting the waitlist and awarding an upgrade for members travelling on Economy Choice fares. This means that the Members requesting the waitlist must either be Platinum or Gold Status at both the time they request to be put on the UpgradeMe Points waitlist and at the time upgrades are awarded from our waitlist, which can occur up until 24 hours prior to departure.
    • If you have registered to waitlist a domestic or short haul international flight Upgrade request prior to 5 days before flight departure (when we commence reviewing and confirming Upgrades from our waitlist), you can continue to check online to see if an Upgrade has become available. If an Upgrade is available, go ahead and book the Upgrade and your waitlist request will be removed.

    • A waitlist request will be cancelled if the booking is cancelled, if you check-in to your flight, if you voluntarily change your booking date or time or if an airline partner change occurs to the flight and the departure or arrival port of the flight is changed.

    • If we secure your Upgrade, an email confirmation will be sent to confirm that your waitlist request was successful and you will receive an updated Flight itinerary and Electronic Ticket Receipt via email. You will only be notified if your waitlist Upgrade request is successful .
    • Economy Lite and Economy Reward Seatfares are not eligible for upgrades.
    • Upgrades in a Virgin Australia domestic Lounge (for domestic and short haul international flights only)
      • Platinum members may request an upgrade from any Virgin Australia Lounge until 30 minutes prior to the scheduled flight departure time, for a flight departing from any domestic airport.
      • Gold, Silver and Red members with Virgin Australia lounge access can request an upgrade fromtwo hours until 30 minutes prior to the scheduled flight departure time, for flights that are departing from the domestic airport the lounge is located in.
    • Points required to upgrade are based on each individual sector shown on an itinerary. If a flight upgrade is requested on two or more individual sectors shown, the Points required will be calculated and quoted separately for each flight sector.
    • The Business Class checked baggage allowance will apply to the upgraded booking. Velocity Platinum, Gold and Silver members are permitted an additional complimentary checked baggage allowance, based on the Business class entitlement.
    • To be eligible for an upgrade, domestic and short haul international flights must be marketed and operated by Virgin Australia and issued by Virgin Australia with a ticket number beginning with 795. Long haul international flights must be flights between Australia and Doha, operated by Qatar Airways with a flight number VA1-29, and issued by Virgin Australia with a ticket number beginning with "795".
    • Upgrade availability is limited and may not be available on all flights. A cancellation and amendment fee may apply.
    • For operational reasons not all Business Class seats are made available for upgrades. This means that there may still be empty seats on departure.
    • Members will earn Status Credits and Points based on the original fare class booked in the lower cabin.
    • The fare rules applicable to the fare originally purchased will apply, regardless of upgraded class of travel.
    • For domestic or short haul international flights, if you have made any special requests for your booking, including e.g. a Mobility or Medical Assistance request, please contact our Membership Contact Centre at least two hours prior to the scheduled flight departure time to request an upgrade.
    • When a domestic or international short haul Business Class upgrade is requested within 3hrs of departurevia our Membership Contact Centre, online, at our airport check-in counter, check-in kiosks, or Virgin Australia Domestic lounges,we may not be able to guarantee you will be provided with a Business Class meal.

    Changes and Cancellations

    Change or cancellation fee: $35 or 4,500 Points per person per booking (waived for Platinum members4)

    Changes and Cancellations are permitted, provided you request it at least 24 hours before a flights scheduled departure and you pay the applicable fee.

    The fare conditions of your originally purchased fare remain applicable in addition to this Upgrade Cancellation and Amendment Policy. Should you choose to cancel or amend your upgraded itinerary, a seat may no longer be available in your originally purchased fare class and you may be required to pay any applicable fare difference.

    If you cancel or change a confirmed upgrade less than 24 hours beforea flights scheduled departure, or fail to board a flight, youll forfeit the Points used to redeem the UpgradeMe Points upgrade5.

    Requests can be made by calling the Membership Contact Centre
