It's easy to keep your Points fresh

Yes Points can expire after 24 months of account inactivity. That means no eligible earnings or redemptions for two whole years.

Every time you earn, redeem or buy Points, you’re refreshing your account balance for another 24 months! A single Point in or a single Point out is all it takes.

  • Points transferred from a Family Pool, Family Points Transfers, and earning Status Credits won’t stop your Points from expiring
  •  Once Points have expired, they cannot be reinstated. Your Points expiry will not result in closure of your Velocity account.

The ‘All Inclusive Fare’ includes base fare, taxes (including GST), fees and carrier charges and excludes seat selection purchase and other extras, such as Economy X, Preferred Seating, Baggage Allowance and Carbon Offset. Members may be eligible to earn additional Velocity Points on eligible flights marketed and operated by Virgin Australia or marketed by Virgin Australia and operated by Link Airways (with a VA flight number) or marketed by Virgin Australia and operated by Air New Zealand (with a VA flight number) in connection with other offers.

6For bookings made from 2 April 2025, Velocity members that have earned Points from spending with an eligible Velocity Points earning credit card within the 12 months prior to flying with Virgin Australia, Link Airways or Air New Zealand will earn 5 Base Points per $1 spent on the All-Inclusive Fare when flying on a Virgin Australia operated flight with a VA flight number, a Link Airways operated flight with a VA flight number, or an Air New Zealand operated flight with a VA flight number. The ‘Base Points’ refers to the minimum number of Points that can be earned by a Red member when flying with Virgin Australia. The ‘All Inclusive Fare’ includes base fare, taxes (including GST), fees and carrier charges and excludes seat selection purchase and other extras, such as Economy X, Preferred Seating, Baggage Allowance or Carbon Offset.
